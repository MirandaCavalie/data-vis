World Happiness Report 2025

This choropleth map shows how happy people are in each country based on the World Happiness Report 2025. The score comes from a life evaluation survey where people rate their lives on a scale from 0 to 10. Darker blue means happier. Hover over any country to see its score.

Source: World Happiness Report 2025 — Wellbeing Research Centre, University of Oxford / Gallup World Poll

Note: Countries in gray have no data available in the World Happiness Report 2025 dataset.
