Displaced 27 Years of Eviction in San Francisco

This project explores 48,000+ eviction notices filed in San Francisco from 1997 to 2026. Using scrollytelling and interactive dashboards built with D3.js, it reveals how displacement patterns shifted across neighborhoods and economic cycles.

Project Title: Displaced: 27 Years of Eviction in San Francisco
Name: Miranda Cavalie
Dataset: SF Rent Board Eviction Notices
Dataset Portal: DataSF
Features completed:
All four static visualizations are done. The choropleth map shows SF's 41 neighborhoods colored by eviction count using D3 and the DataSF GeoJSON. The line chart shows total evictions per year from 1997 to 2025 with annotations for the dot-com peak and COVID drop. The stacked area chart breaks down evictions by reason over time. The horizontal bar chart ranks the top 10 neighborhoods. The map and line chart are built in D3.js.
