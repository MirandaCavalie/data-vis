A7 – Time Series Visualization in D3

Dataset: PJM Energy Consumption — Daily Averages (~400 daily records)
Context:Hourly energy consumption (in MW) from 4 U.S. regions operated by PJM Interconnection: COMED (Illinois), FE (Ohio), DEOK (Ohio/Kentucky), and PJMW (Western PJM).
Source: PJM Interconnection LLC — hourly electricity load data for 4 regional transmission zones.

D3.js Time Series Charts

Chart Type Link
Multi-Line Chart 4 lines, one per region d3.scaleTime() Open Multi-Line
Overlapping Area Chart 4 overlapping areas with opacity Open Area Chart
Small Multiples 2×2 grid of line charts, one per region Open Small Multiples
Streamgraph d3.stackOffsetWiggle stacked stream layout Open Streamgraph

All charts include interactive tooltips on hover and color legends via Susie Lu's d3-legend.
